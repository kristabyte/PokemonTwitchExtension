Pokopia Pokédex — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Pokopia Pokédex is a Twitch Extension that displays a viewer's Pokémon collection on a broadcaster's channel. This privacy policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

What Data Is Collected

When you share your Twitch identity with the extension, the following information is accessed:

Twitch User ID and Username: Used to look up and display your Pokémon collection. Your Twitch user ID is mapped to your username and stored in a Firebase database so the extension can identify you on future visits.

Pokémon Collection Data: The Pokémon you have caught, including species, catch counts, and shiny status. This data is created through the broadcaster's StreamElements chat widget when you use catch commands or channel point redemptions.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to display your Pokémon collection within the Twitch Extension panel. Data is stored per-channel, meaning your collection on one broadcaster's channel is separate from another's.

Data Storage

Collection data is stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database. Data is organized by channel and username. No passwords, email addresses, payment information, or other sensitive personal data is collected or stored.

Data Sharing

Your Pokémon collection data is visible to other viewers who use the "Search Trainer" feature within the extension on the same channel. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as the broadcaster uses the Pokopia Pokédex system. If you would like your data removed, please contact the broadcaster or the extension developer.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only data collected is your public Twitch username and in-game Pokémon collection data.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the extension developer through Twitch at twitch.tv/kristabyte.
